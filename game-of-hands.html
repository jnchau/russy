Game of Hands

Summer 2017

I found I can name the handedness of nearly every NHL player off the top of my head.
So naturally, I designed an internet game to test that skill.
For hockey fans, it's a game of talent.
For everyone else, it's a 50/50 chance.

I then mailed challenge letters to some of our continent’s most prestigious hockey minds.
You can read those lower on this page.













Play on Game of Hands dot net.
