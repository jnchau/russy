Minnesota Wild (produced work)

I spent last summer in Minneapolis as the copywriting intern at Olson.
Our small creative group competed in a pitch for the Minnesota Wild 2018 season campaign.
I helped concept and create this new hockey tradition perfect for Minnesotans.

Having played hockey since I could walk, this was a fitting experience.


This is Our Ice

Invite fans to bring water from lakes, ponds, and backyard rinks all over the state of Minnesota and add them to the ice surface at the Xcel Energy Center, home of the NHL's Minnesota Wild. Check out the 90-second breakdown below.



Preseason Fan Event

The campaign launched with an event to collect water donations.
This recently won gold at AdFed's The Show 2018 in Minneapolis, MN.



Interactive Digital Map

This online map lets fans share the location they brought water from, and what that ice means to them.



Minnesota Hockey Legends

Local hockey heroes talk about the importance of the ice they grew up on. They then conducted a pouring ceremony to christen the ice before the season's first home game.



Rabbit From Super Troopers

Actor/writer/producer Eric Stolhanske is a Minnesota native. This is him yelling the tagline at a Wild game, in full character.
