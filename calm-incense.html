Calm Incense

Fall 2017

I’ve recently taken a liking to the calming effect of burning incense.
And I’ve always been a fan of common sense.
Last summer, while on a plane from Minnesota to LA, I scribbled this pun.
Months later, Eddy Allen and I brought it to life.


What is Calm Incense?

A series of wooden, hand-sanded incense holders with statements of common sense carved into them.


Why does it exist?

Far too many people ignore these commandments of common sense.
Gifting someone a Calm Incense holder may be the perfectly subtle reminder they need.
